Andrew's Paws and Palms Bakery

Overview

Welcome to Andrew's Paws and Palms Bakery! We are a small business that bake and sell dog treats and baked goods for people. We are located in Hanover, PA USA in a residential home. The business started out with doing some personal baking for myself and then I decided to try making dog treats for my dog, Zoey. I started making dog treats for Zoey because I wanted to make sure that she was eating healthy and safe treats.
I informed myself and to others that I would start taking on baking when I moved out of my parents house in March 2023. I had a lot of people support my decision this is especially true for my parents. In fact, my step-mother, Tiffany, was the one who kick started the whole process by surprising me with a book ,via delivery, about bread making and now I was not the only one invested into my baking decision. Mrs. Tiff has elminated the hardest part for me, knowing where to start. I am very grateful for her and my parents for supporting me in my decision.
The first thing I started baking was bread, and more specifically the first bread being 'Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Loaves'. It was simple to make and contained some of my favorite ingredients. The bread was delicious and I was very proud of myself for making it. I was so proud of myself that I decided to share it with my friends and family. I received a lot of positive feedback from them and they encouraged me to continue baking and so I did. My next bread was 'Irish Soda Bread', this is where I had my first failure. I misread the recipe and swapped the amounts of sugar and salt. The bread was so salty that it was inedible. I was very shocked at first but I was not discouraged. I laughed it off and tried again. I was determined to make the bread and I did. I was very proud of myself for not giving up and I was very happy with the results. I shared the bread with my family and they loved it.
After several successful breads, Mrs. Tiff surprised me again with a book on cakes. I was very excited to start baking cakes and I started with a simple vanilla cake. This is where I had my second failure. The cake pan that I had was a detachable pan and I never thought to put it on a baking sheet. The cake batter leaked out of the pan and onto the bottom of the oven. I was very upset with myself but I was not discouraged. I cleaned up the mess and tried again. I was determined to make the cake and I did. My second attempt I put a rectangular baking sheet in the round pan and it did work, there was no leakage; however, the cake was not smoothly round due to the baking sheet. I was very proud of myself for not giving up and I was very happy with the results. On my third attempt, I had finally put the cake pan on a baking sheet and the cake was perfectly round. I made homemade whipped vanilla frosting to cover the cake with. I shared the cake with my family and they loved. For Mother's Day I made a 'Cinnimon Spice' cake for my mother and decorated it using my new decorating kit that I had gotten after my third cake. Despite my beginner skills in cake decorating, it actually turned out pretty good. My mother loved the cake and she was very proud of me.

The Team

Founder

Andrew

Andrew is the esteemed owner and founder of Paws and Palms Bakery, a delightful establishment that celebrates the harmonious blend of culinary artistry and heartfelt adoration for our beloved canine friends. With a distinguished academic background, Andrew holds a Bachelor's degree in Web Development from the esteemed Capitol Technology University. An unwavering affection for dogs has always been a driving force in Andrew's life, epitomized by his faithful companion, Zoey. Inspired by his profound love for his furry friend, Andrew has channeled his passion for baking and cooking into a purposeful venture.
