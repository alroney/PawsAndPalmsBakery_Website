Dog Biscuits/Training-Treat Disclaimer
  • Due to the ratio between the whole wheat flour and other ingredient(s) in the biscuits, they all look very similar coming out of the oven.

  • We can not guarantee that the biscuits will look exactly like the pictures.

  • All of our dog biscuits are not intended for human consumption. Trust me you won't taste anything, but your dog definitely will due to their enhanced senses.

  • Upon ordering please specify the level of softness/hardness you would like your biscuits to be. If you do not specify, they will be made to the standard level of softness/hardness.

  • The softer the treat, the sooner they expire. This is caused by the moisture still in the treat. Softer treats can last about a week in a container outside of the fridge. Hard treats can last a month outside of the fridge.
