Cookie Policy

At Paws and Palms Bakery, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies on our website and online services and outlines your options regarding the use of cookies.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to enhance your browsing experience, provide certain functionalities, and collect information about your usage of the website.

Our Use of Cookies
At Paws and Palms Bakery, we prioritize your privacy and do not use any cookies to track browser information. We do not employ any tracking cookies that collect personally identifiable information or gather data for advertising purposes. The cookies we may use on our website are strictly necessary cookies. These cookies are essential for the operation of our website and enable you to navigate and use its features. They are typically set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms. You may choose to disable these cookies through your browser settings, but please note that doing so may impact the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies
We do not voluntarily use any third-party cookies on our website for tracking or analytical purposes. We strive to maintain a cookie-free environment that respects your privacy and ensures a secure browsing experience. Your browser may be using cookies to store information from third-party websites that you have visited. These cookies are set by the third-party website and are not controlled by us. We do not have access to these cookies and cannot control how they are used. You should refer to the third-party website's privacy policy for more information on how they use cookies.

Managing Your Cookies Preferences
As we do not use any cookies to track browser information, there are no specific cookie preferences to manage for our website. However, you may still control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to accept or reject cookies, as well as delete cookies that have already been stored on your device. Please refer to your browser's help documentation or settings for more information on how to adjust your cookie preferences.

Updates to this Cookie Policy
We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect any changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we use cookies.
